Lesson 3 (Live Analysis) 17/08/2023

Focus Areas

  • During full saves, I recommend picking angles that are super close range which makes it so getting a headshot with your pistol does max damage as well as making the headshot easier to get, I also recommend often overlooked positions as this would sometimes allow you to get overlooked.
  • LOS utility while standing in the open is dangerous and can often get you killed.
  • Don't cross long in the same peek you throw the long molly as this leaves you open for about 2 seconds after dropping the utility in which you can get punished by enemies rushing.
  • When you crouch and there are multiple enemies on your screen is guarantees your death as it makes movement extremely slow and makes the job for the enemies of lining up a headshot much easier as well as telling them your intensions.
  • Get more comfortable making big swipes as currently your large swipes are still a bit inaccurate and you can't consistently hit a 90 degree or a 180 flick.

Lesson 4 (Live Analysis) 22/08/2023

Focus Areas

  • Pistol rounds are crucial to winning and I always recommend at least buying body armour on these rounds.
  • LOS utility, exposing yourself to angles if the enemy gets a decent timing they could peek you while you have utility pulled.
  • Reloading while vulnerable to a push or peek from an enemy happened often this game.
  • Try to follow through with your engagements a bit more as if the first part of your spray doesn't feel right you will often stop shooting to reset and this got you killed a couple times.
  • Full buys are the most important rounds of the games, and as such I want you to always buy full utility on these rounds even if that brings your bank to $0 because every piece of utility can incrementally increase your chances of winning the round. “It's better to have it and not need it than to need it and not have it.” ― George Ellis
  • Last round of the half always use every dollar you can by buying full utility and a secondary as saving money is a waste.
