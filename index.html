Lesson 1 (Introduction Lesson/Live Analysis) 25/07/2023

Focus Areas

  • Try to only engage with one enemy at one time, peeking multiple enemies significantly reduces your chances of survival and getting a kill.
  • Sometimes accidentally shooting before you character is completely still this results in your first few bullets being inaccurate, often getting you killed.
  • Use your minimap when you teammates are not communicating effectively this is a hard skill to develop but the sooner you develop it the more you can benefit from it.
  • Low sens getting you in trouble a few times when an enemy was outside of your comfortable range although fortuantely it didn't effect you to badly.
  • When picking positions, some of the deciding factors should be: Good cover? Headshot angle? Can I fall back? at times during this match your positioning wasn't ideal exposing yourself to multiple opponents at once without good cover.
