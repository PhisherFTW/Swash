FFA Community-Deathmatch Notes

  • Tapping in certain situations feels uncomfortable and not the preferred shooting style
  • spraying seems very comfortable but generally more confident at medium ranges
  • Spraying at close ranges is sometimes overexaggerated which could be contributed to panic and pulldown at close range is a bit slow leading to a few bullets going over the enemy sometimes
  • Sometimes shooting before properly counter-strafing.
  • Sometimes shooting before the crosshair is on the enemy this leads to having to adjust while spraying which is more challenging.
  • Sometimes shooting mid flick or micro-adjustment instead of waiting for the end.
